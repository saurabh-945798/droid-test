Description Teaching Faculty Goals

As the President of the Droid Club, my duty is to foster a community that promotes the exchange of knowledge and innovation, and that supports the interest of members in all things technology-related. One of the core areas of interest for members of the club is 3D modeling, design, and manufacturing, and I am excited to announce that we will be offering a series of workshops on the 3DExperience platform to members and upcoming students. The 3DExperience platform is a state-of-the-art cloud-based digital platform that provides a comprehensive solution for enterprises and individuals to manage product design, development, and manufacturing processes. By offering training on this platform, we will give members the resources and skills to become proficient users of the latest technology in product design and development. ..
