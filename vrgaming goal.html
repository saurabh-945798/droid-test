3D VR Gaming

Description Teaching Faculty Goals

As the Vice President of the Droid Club, I am excited to announce our upcoming workshop on 3D VR Gaming. This workshop aims to introduce participants to the fascinating world of 3D VR gaming and provide them with a thorough understanding of the technology, techniques, and tools used to create compelling and immersive gaming experiences. The goals of the workshop are as follows: Understanding 3D VR Gaming Technology: We aim to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental principles behind 3D VR gaming technology, including the various hardware components, software requirements, and techniques for building and creating 3D VR games. Creating A 3D VR Environment: Participants will get hands-on experience on how to create a 3D VR environment using tools such as Unity and Unreal Engine. They will learn how to use 3D modeling tools such as Blender to create 3D objects and import them into a 3D game environment. Game Design and Mechanics: The workshop will cover the basics of game design and mechanics as they relate to 3D VR gaming.
